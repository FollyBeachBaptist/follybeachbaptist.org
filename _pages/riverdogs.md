## ⚾ Faith & Family Night at the Joe

Folly Beach Baptist Church invites you to join us for a special evening with the **Charleston RiverDogs** on **Saturday, August 22**.

Admission is **FREE** for everyone who registers.

### Event Highlights

- Gates open at **5:00 p.m.**
- Meet the RiverDogs players
- Pray with the players
- Hear an inspiring Christian testimony from a RiverDogs team member
- Enjoy an exciting evening of baseball and fellowship

Whether you're coming with family, friends, or by yourself, we'd love to have you join us!
