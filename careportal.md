# CarePortal Training & Information Session

Folly Beach Baptist Church is partnering with **CarePortal**, a ministry that connects local churches with real-time needs of children and families in our community.

By meeting practical needs such as beds, clothing, food, and other essentials, churches have the opportunity to share the love of Christ in tangible ways while helping strengthen families.
